About Company:

Infosys Limited is a multinational information technology company that provides business consulting, information technology and outsourcing services. Infosys is a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting. The company was founded in Pune and is headquarted in Bangalore. Infosys is the second - largest Indian company. One of it's known product is Finacle which is an universal banking solution. Infosys provides software development, maintenance and independent validation services to companies in finance, insurance, manufacturing and other domains.
